BMW i5 eDrive40

The BMW i5 eDrive40 is a rear-wheel-drive electric sedan with 340 horsepower, a 0-100 km/h time of 6 seconds, and an 81 kWh usable battery. It features a single rear-mounted electric motor, offers an estimated range of up to 270-295 miles, and can fast-charge from 10% to 80% in about 30 minutes. Key features include a comfortable ride, modern interior with a curved display, and a high level of technology.
